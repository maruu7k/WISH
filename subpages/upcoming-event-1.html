Toy Drive Gala

Join us for an elegant evening of dinner, dancing, and giving, all in support of our mission to provide educational toys to underprivileged children. The Toy Drive Gala promises to be a night of glamour and goodwill, where every moment is dedicated to making a meaningful difference.

As the stars twinkle overhead, we'll gather at the enchanting venue of Mississauga Square One Mall on the 10th of December 2023. The mall's atrium will be transformed into a haven of elegance, where guests will be welcomed by the warm embrace of holiday spirit.

The evening will commence with a delectable dinner, carefully curated to delight the senses and set the stage for a night of celebration. Savory flavors and sweet indulgences will be accompanied by the joyous hum of conversation, as like-minded individuals come together to support a shared cause.

As the music fills the air, the dance floor will beckon, inviting guests to waltz and sway to the rhythm of unity. Laughter and camaraderie will flow freely, creating memories that will linger long after the night has come to a close.

But the true highlight of the evening will be the act of giving itself. Guests will have the opportunity to contribute to our mission, knowing that their generosity will directly impact the lives of children in need. Every donation, whether a toy or a financial contribution, will be a step towards narrowing the educational gap and fostering a love for learning.

The Toy Drive Gala is not just an event; it's a promise of brighter tomorrows for children who deserve every opportunity to thrive. As the clock strikes midnight, we'll bid farewell, knowing that together, we've ignited a spark of hope in young hearts.

Save the date - December 10th, 2023, at Mississauga Square One Mall. Let's come together and create a legacy of learning, one toy at a time.
