Our Story

The genesis of ‘Wish’ was rooted in a collective belief that every child, regardless of their circumstances, should have the opportunity to experience the joys of play intertwined with valuable learning experiences. This vision emerged from the profound understanding that well-chosen educational toys can be powerful tools in a child's development.

The name ‘Wish’ originates from our guiding motto - ‘Tend to Wish’. This slogan encapsulates our mission to nurture dreams and potential through play. It embodies our dedication to creating opportunities for children to learn, grow, and, most importantly, to dream.

Motivated by this insight, a dedicated group of individuals came together to form ‘Wish’. Their mission was clear: to provide educational toys to children who might not have access to them otherwise. This initiative became a beacon of hope, offering a gateway to learning and growth for countless young minds.

As the word spread about our mission, a community of supporters rallied behind us. Some generously contributed financial resources to procure more educational toys, while others volunteered their time and expertise to ensure the toys reached the hands of deserving children.

Today, ‘Wish’ stands as a testament to the impact that can be achieved through collective action. Each donated toy, every financial contribution, and the invaluable hours invested by our dedicated volunteers have collectively made a profound difference in the lives of many children.

Reflecting on this journey, ‘Wish’ remains resolute in its commitment to fostering growth and learning through play. This narrative is not just a reflection of our progress, but a solemn pledge to the children we serve - affirming that their potential is boundless, and we are here to support their journey towards a brighter future.
