Toy Donation Guidelines

“Unlocking Learning, One Toy at a Time”

We greatly appreciate your interest in contributing to our cause by donating educational toys. Your generosity plays a pivotal role in brightening the educational journey of underprivileged children. To ensure that each toy makes a meaningful impact, we kindly request that you adhere to the following guidelines:

  1. Types of Toys: We focus on educational toys that encourage learning through play. This may include puzzles, building blocks, interactive games, art supplies, and age-appropriate books.
  2. Age-Appropriateness: Please consider the age group of the children we serve. Toys suitable for ages 3 to 12 are highly encouraged.
  3. Quality Assurance: We value the durability and safety of toys. Kindly ensure that donated toys are in good condition, with all parts intact and free from any safety hazards.
  4. Packaging and Shipping: If you're mailing toys, secure packaging is essential to prevent any damage during transit. Please include a note with your contact information for our gratitude.
